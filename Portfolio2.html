Nipah


Discover the best of local, pan-Asian and Western cuisine with a delicious difference. Previously known as Nipah Coffee Shop at the old Hotel Equatorial Kuala Lumpur, the brand new Nipah is nothing like the old coffee house. The same popular dishes have been recreated with a rich variety and history. From classic local Malaysian dishes like freshly made upon request roti canai and satay to Western favourites like customised pizza and pasta, we cover all bases, even decadent desserts desserts of all colours, shapes and sizes. Don’t miss out on the sumptuous lunch and dinner with unlimited servings available daily! Takeaway, delivery and drive-through available during the Full Movement Control Order (FMCO).

Kin Thai


Kin Thai Restaurant was established in 2018 by a team of Thai food enthusiasts who wanted to share their passion for Thai cuisine with Malaysians. The restaurant quickly gained a following for its delicious food and warm hospitality.

Some of the must-try dishes at Kin Thai Restaurant include the Tom Yum soup, which is made with fresh lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal. The Pad Thai is another popular dish, made with rice noodles, prawns, tofu, peanuts, and bean sprouts. The Green Curry with chicken is also a must-try, made with fresh green chili, basil, and coconut milk.

At Kin Thai Restaurant, you can expect exceptional service from their friendly and knowledgeable staff. They are happy to provide recommendations and ensure that your dining experience is a memorable one.
